Hypertrophy

Through ancient times like Ancient greece, people have searched for ways to improve their physique and strength. The process of gaining muscle by working out is called hypertrophy, this process requires high quantites of energy and proteins, to gain huge amount of muscle its recommended to fat up and consume high quantities of proteins combined with excersises that target the muscle you want to grow. The most effective way to gain muscle is to do compound exercises like squats, deadlifts, bench press, and pull ups.

Food and Diet

To gain muscle it is recommended to do a fat up process, in which you may even double your caloric intake, in order to have enough energy instored for your body to build up muscle, and to eat at las 1.8 grams of protein per kilogram of body weight to ensure a healthy and lasting muscle gain, and to eat a lot of carbs to have enough energy for your workouts, and to eat healthy fats to ensure a healthy body and a good hormonal balance.

Supplements

Theres a massive amount of supplements in the market, but the most effective ones are creatine, whey protein, and caffeine, creatine is a substance that is found naturally in the body and it helps to increase strength and muscle mass, whey protein is a fast digesting protein that helps to build muscle, and caffeine is a stimulant that helps to increase energy and focus during workouts. There are many hormonal supplements that can be hazardous for the user health and are illegal or regulated in many countries, they are not recommended and even less for begginers.

Class Schedule

To gain muscle it is recommended to workout at least 4 times a week, and to target each muscle group at least twice a week, and to have at least one day of rest between workouts, and to have a good sleep schedule to ensure a good recovery and muscle growth.
